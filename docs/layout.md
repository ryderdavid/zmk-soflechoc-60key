## Encoder Positions

| Encoder | Position | Default (BASE) |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| Left | 42 | `[` (bracket) |
| Right | 43 | `]` (bracket) |

Encoder rotation is configured separately via `sensor-bindings`:
- **BASE**: Left=Volume Up/Down, Right=Page Up/Down
- **LOWER**: Left=Arrow Left/Right, Right=Arrow Up/Down

## Split Halves

- **Left half** (positions 0-5, 12-17, 24-29, 36-42, 50-54): 30 keys
- **Right half** (positions 6-11, 18-23, 30-35, 43-49, 55-59): 30 keys
- Left half is the central (USB-connected) half by default
